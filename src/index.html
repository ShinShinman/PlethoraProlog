    about

    PROLOG

    was founded in 2017. PROLOG engages with architecture through design projects, research, writing, exhibitions and workshops. PROLOG aims to frame each project from a design and theoretical perspective. PROLOG is thought as a formative period, therefore its focus lies in the exploration, speculations and experiments. PROLOG is scattered around Europe with a base in Wroclaw, Poland.

    PROLOG is a curator of 'Trouble in Paradise' the exhibition for Polish Pavilion at the Biennale Architettura 2021.
